Maori Hill House
Residential

The project involved the renovation of the existing kitchen and dining spaces and improving access to the adjoining living room. The living room was already open to the dining room, but not to the kitchen so this wall was partially removed to ceiling height in sympathy with the existing architecture. A wall separated the kitchen from the dining room and this was removed entirely to improve light and the sense of space. A new contemporary kitchen was then installed.
